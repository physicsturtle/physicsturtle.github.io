$$\begin{align}
\frac{\d}{\d x} \sinh x ={}& \cosh x \\
\frac{\d}{\d x} \cosh x ={}& \sinh x \\
\frac{\d}{\d x} \tanh x ={}& \sech^2 x \\
\frac{\d}{\d x} \csch x ={}& -\coth x \csch x \\
\frac{\d}{\d x} \sech x ={}& -\tanh x \sech x \\
\frac{\d}{\d x} \coth x ={}& -\csch^2 x
\end{align}$$
